Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Account credentials (passwords are encrypted)
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Event registrations and volunteer history
  • Organization details (for organizers)
  • Support messages and communications

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process event registrations and manage volunteer activities
  • Send you important updates about your account and events
  • Generate certificates and track volunteer hours
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With event organizers when you register for their events
  • For certificate verification (public verification IDs only)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With service providers who assist in operating our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted passwords using secure hashing algorithms
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure database storage with regular backups

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Export your volunteer history and certificates

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to maintain your session and improve your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

VolunteerSync is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Safety Guidelines

The safety of all users is our top priority. These guidelines help ensure a safe and positive experience for everyone.

1. Event Safety

Organizers are responsible for:

  • Conducting safety assessments before events
  • Providing clear safety instructions to volunteers
  • Ensuring adequate supervision and first aid resources
  • Following all applicable safety regulations and laws
  • Having emergency action plans in place

2. Volunteer Safety

Volunteers should:

  • Follow all safety instructions provided by organizers
  • Report any safety concerns immediately
  • Wear appropriate clothing and safety equipment
  • Stay within designated work areas
  • Notify organizers of any medical conditions or limitations

3. Online Safety

To protect yourself online:

  • Never share your password with anyone
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Be cautious when sharing personal information
  • Report suspicious activity or accounts
  • Log out when using shared devices

4. Reporting Concerns

If you encounter safety issues or inappropriate behavior:

  • Report immediately to event organizers or support
  • Contact support@volunteersync.org for urgent matters
  • Document incidents with photos or notes when safe to do so
  • Contact local authorities for emergencies

5. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, discrimination, or abuse of any kind
  • Physical violence or threats
  • Sexual harassment or inappropriate conduct
  • Substance abuse at events
  • Any illegal activities

6. Consequences

Violations of safety guidelines may result in:

  • Immediate removal from events
  • Suspension or termination of accounts
  • Legal action when appropriate
  • Reporting to relevant authorities

Data Protection

We are committed to protecting your data through industry-standard security practices and responsible data management.

1. Data Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted:

  • Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms (never stored in plain text)
  • Data transmission is protected with HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database connections are secured

2. Access Controls

We implement strict access controls:

  • Role-based access (admin, organizer, volunteer)
  • Authentication required for all sensitive operations
  • Regular access audits and reviews
  • Limited access to personal information

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely:

  • In secure, monitored databases
  • With regular automated backups
  • Following data retention policies
  • In compliance with applicable regulations

4. Incident Response

In the event of a data breach:

  • We will notify affected users promptly
  • We will take immediate steps to contain and address the issue
  • We will comply with all legal notification requirements
  • We will work to prevent future incidents

5. Third-Party Services

We carefully vet third-party services and require them to:

  • Maintain appropriate security standards
  • Comply with data protection agreements
  • Use data only for specified purposes

6. Your Data Rights

You can:

  • Access all data we have about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your data in a portable format

7. Contact Us

For data protection inquiries, contact:

  • Email: support@volunteersync.org
  • Subject: "Data Protection Request"
