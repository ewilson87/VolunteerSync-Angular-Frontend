Frequently asked questions

How quickly does VolunteerSync respond to support requests?

We respond to most inquiries within 24 hours on business days. Organizer priority requests receive a same-business-day follow-up.

Where can I see the status of my submitted events?

Organizers can view pending, approved, and completed events directly in the organizer dashboard. You’ll also receive email updates when an event status changes.

How do volunteers track their verified hours?

Volunteer hours appear in your profile once the event organizer confirms attendance. You can export a certificate at any time from the “My Impact” tab.
